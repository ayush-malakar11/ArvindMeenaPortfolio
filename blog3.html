COMPANY FRESH START SCHEME,2020

Applicability

Any company which has made a default in filing of any of the documents,statements, returns, etc including annual statutory documents with MCA registry is permitted to file belated documents, under this Scheme.

Additional Fee/Penalty

There shall be no additional fee/Penalty on the delay filing of the documents under this scheme; only normal fee prescribed under the act shall be required to pay by the defaulting companies.

Time duration for avail the scheme

From 01st April, 2020 to 30th September, 2020.

Immunity from prosecution in respect of document(s) filed under the scheme

The defaulting companies, which have filed their pending documents under the scheme and made good the default, shall not be subjected to prosecution by Registrar for such defaults. The application for seeking immunity in respect of belated documents filed under the scheme may be made in the E Form CFSS2020, from 01st October, 2020 to 31st March, 2021.

Scheme shall not apply

a) To companies against which action for final notice for striking off the name u/s
248 of the act has already been initiated by the ROC;
b) Where any application (Form STK-2) has already been filed by the companies for action of striking off the name of the company;
c) To companies which have amalgamated under a scheme of arrangements or compromise under the act;
d) Where application have already been filed for obtaining dormant status under section 455 of the act before this scheme;
e) To vanishing companies;
f) Where any increase in authorized capital is involved (Form SH-7) and also charge related documents (CHG-1, CHG-4, CHG-8 and CHG-9)
