About Us

Established in 2015, Arvind Meena & Associates is a Indore based practicing company Secretary firm registered with the Institute of Com­pany Secretaries of India (ICSI) & Proprietored by Mr. Arvind Kumar Meena.

Our Specialized Services In Area Of Company Formation, Startup Funding,Corporate Laws, Maintenance Of Secretarial Records And Statutory Register, MCA Services, NCLT Services, Intellectual Property Rights, Corporate Governance Issues, Legal Drafting of Agreements, Listing Compliance, RBI Compliance, Audits, • Banking And Finance Sector, Etc.

About Proprietor

Mr. Arvind Kumar Meena is a Fellow Member of the ICSI and also a Commerce graduate from University of Rajasthan. He has secured AIR-3 in Company Law Quiz Competition-2014 conducted by ICSI and having Five years experience in the field of corporate law consultancy, secretarial & compliance management services.

He is also empanelled with Independent director data bank maintained by Ministry of Corporate Affairs. He has immense knowledge and analytical approach in dealing with matters related to Company Law, Securities Law, RBI Compliances, Secretarial Audit and Due Diligence Services. In Arvind Meena & Associates he has successfully completed an assignment of conversion of LLP to Private Limited Company (First in Madhya Pradesh) also successfully completed Secretarial Compliance relating to Private Equity and Venture Capitalist Funding from various investors e.g. FK Myntra, Info Edge, Siana Capital, Madhya Pradesh Venture Finance Limited (MPVFL) in various Client Companies.
